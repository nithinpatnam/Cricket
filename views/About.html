About Dhoni Academy

M.S.Dhoni Cricket Academies’ unique and ground breaking coaching program is built around the core values, with which our mentor M.S.Dhoni lives and plays by; INTEGRITY, TEAMWORK, ENJOYMENT, PROFESSIONALISM, & ADAPTABILITY. Coaching at MSDCA imparts a lot of game sense and practical activities, with its focus on developing a participant’s ability to perform during a match and not just in the nets. MSDCA Cricket Coaching Program will promote the importance of developing quality cricketers as well as people who are leaders, have strong team work capabilities, strive for excellence and display sportsmanship and fair play. We, at MSDCA, aspire to be the world leader in developing a new generation of players and coaches. We are fully equipped technically, physically, and mentally to meet the demands of all formats of the game, through the core values adopted by our mentor M.S. Dhoni. As a team we aspire to design and deliver a distinctive, high value and scalable program that transforms world cricket. Vision:- Inclusive growth and equal opportunities for all Cricketors across all Geographies. Mission:- Provide Scientific Coaching, Competitive exposure and create Employment opportunities for all Upcoming and Ex Cricketors.
